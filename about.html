  • Our Mission

    Our primary mission is to help mid-market companies shift their limited financial and human resources away from managing their aging, inefficient, and expensive to maintain legacy insurance software applications, so they can invest those same dollars and people into transforming their business for the future.

    • We do that by working with our clients to minimize the cost of legacy application maintenance while ensuring superior levels of client satisfaction.

  • According to Gartner

    According to Gartner, a highly respected IT research firm, many companies are spending 50 to 80 percent of their IT budgets on maintaining legacy applications.

  • Some common challenges we see with legacy system management today

    • Difficulty finding and hiring qualified IT staff to maintain legacy applications
    • Increased costs associated with hiring more IT resources to manage aging systems
    • Increased wages due to the niche skillset required to support legacy systems
    • Lack of cost-savings measures and increased inefficiencies
    • Increased reliance on a legacy software vendor
    • Loss of productivity due to legacy system limitations in a mobile environment
    • Risk of downtime due to system failure
    • Increased recruiting and training costs
    • Increased integration costs and time to integrate legacy system with new systems
    • Lack of innovation to keep up with mobile and Cloud technology, and customer demand
    • Increased time and effort to ensure security and compliance mandates are met

  • We help mid-market insurance companies

    • Manage their legacy systems as efficiently and effectively as possible
    • Reduce the legacy system maintenance spend with the use of low risk and proven application maintenance and production support solutions we have available
    • Focus their financial resources and best people on transforming their IT systems to take advantage of modern, configurable, and highly flexible solutions, while we manage the legacy systems
    • Improve their overall ROI for IT….by reducing the overall spend required to support the legacy applications environment by 30 to 50 percent, the savings can be applied to funding the future of the business, rather than its past

Did you Know ?

According to knowledgeable industry experts, many insurance companies are spending 50 to 80 percent of their IT budgets just to maintain the legacy software applications they installed in the past 20 years.
