About InsWorkz

We provide IT services and business transformation support to mid-market insurance carriers and have expertise across Personal Lines, Commercial Lines, and Specialty products.

Our Mission

Our primary mission is to help mid-market companies by providing low-risk, high-value solutions to reducing the cost of managing older, inefficient, and expensive-to-maintain legacy insurance software applications. We believe that the majority of your IT budget and resources should be focused on building the digital future, not managing the legacy past.

Our Services

Our services include legacy core system application management, testing/QA, Digital transformation, cloud enablement and more!

Our Leadership
